CounselNote — End User Licence (draft, pre-launch)

This is a provisional licence summary for evaluation and pilot use. It is not
a substitute for legal advice and should be reviewed by the purchasing
school or trust before commercial rollout.

1. Grant
   Subject to payment of the applicable fee, the purchaser is granted a
   non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to install and use CounselNote on
   the number of devices stated in the relevant pricing tier, for the
   licence period purchased.

2. Data
   CounselNote stores all pupil and session data locally, encrypted with a
   passphrase chosen by the user. The vendor does not have access to, and
   cannot recover, this data or the passphrase. The purchaser is responsible
   for backups, device security and compliance with UK GDPR and the
   school's own data protection and retention policies.

3. No statutory safeguarding system
   CounselNote is a private counselling workspace and does not replace the
   school's approved child-protection / safeguarding recording system.
   Safeguarding concerns must still be recorded through the school's
   official procedure.

4. Support and updates
   Support and update entitlements are as described for the purchased
   pricing tier in PRICING.md.

5. Liability
   CounselNote is provided "as is". To the maximum extent permitted by
   law, the vendor's liability is limited to the fees paid for the licence
   in the 12 months preceding the claim. Nothing in this licence limits
   liability for death, personal injury, or fraud where such limitation is
   not permitted by law.

6. Governing law
   This licence is governed by the law of England and Wales.

Replace this draft with solicitor-reviewed terms before taking payment from
