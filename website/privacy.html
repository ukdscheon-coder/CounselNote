Website privacy notice

This covers data collected by the CounselNote website and checkout pages — it is separate from the CounselNote app itself, which stores pupil data only on your own computer.

What this site collects

  • Contact form / sales emails: name, email address, school name, and anything you choose to tell us, used only to respond to your enquiry and to administer any purchase.
  • Checkout (Paddle/PayPal): payment is handled entirely by Paddle.com Market Limited or PayPal; we never see or store your card details. We receive your email address and purchase details to issue your licence key.
  • Download page: standard hosting access logs (IP address, browser type) may be recorded by our hosting provider under its own privacy statement, separate from this one.
  • No cookies or tracking pixels are deliberately added by this site.

What this site does NOT collect

  • No pupil data of any kind — this website never has access to anything inside the CounselNote app, which runs entirely offline on your own computer.
  • No marketing cookies, no third-party ad trackers.

Legal basis

  • Responding to enquiries: legitimate interest in providing the information you asked for.
  • Processing a purchase: necessary to perform the contract of sale.

Retention

  • Sales enquiry emails: kept for as long as reasonably needed to support your purchase and licence, then deleted.
  • Purchase records: kept as required for UK tax/accounting obligations (currently 6 years).

Your rights

You can ask what personal data we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it, by emailing privacy@counselnote.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk) if you're unhappy with how your data has been handled.
