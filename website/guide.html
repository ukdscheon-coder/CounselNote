Guide

1. Installation

System requirements

  • Windows 10 or 11, 64-bit
  • No internet connection required after download
  • A modern browser to display CounselNote (Edge or Chrome, already included with Windows)

Steps

  1. Download CounselNote-Windows.zip from the download page.
  2. Right-click the zip and choose Extract All. Choose a permanent location such as Documents\CounselNote — don't run it from inside Downloads.
  3. Open the extracted folder and double-click Launch-CounselNote.bat.
  4. Windows may show "Windows protected your PC" — this is expected for new, unsigned software. Click More info, then Run anyway.
  5. A small window will open showing CounselNote is running at http://127.0.0.1:8790, and your browser will open automatically.

Keep that small window open in the background while you use CounselNote — it can be minimised, but closing it ends your session.

2. First run: creating your vault

  1. On first launch, create a vault passphrase of at least 10 characters.
  2. Choose something you'll remember reliably — there is no password reset. No copy of your passphrase is stored anywhere, including by us, so a forgotten passphrase cannot be recovered.
  3. Write it down and store it securely and separately from the computer (a password manager, or a locked drawer).

4. Safeguarding workflow

CounselNote is a private counselling workspace, not a replacement for your school's statutory child-protection system. When a concern arises:

  1. Select the appropriate risk level and record the evidence behind it.
  2. Record the immediate safety action taken.
  3. Record the decision reached and your rationale.
  4. Use Prepare DSL summary to generate a minimum-necessary summary.
  5. Share that summary with your DSL, and record the concern in your school's approved child-protection system without delay — CounselNote does not do this automatically.

5. Backup and restore

Backup: click Encrypted backup in the sidebar. A single encrypted file downloads; store it somewhere secure and separate from the computer. Back up at least weekly, and always before reinstalling Windows or changing computers.

Restore: on a fresh installation, choose Restore an encrypted vault on the lock screen, select your backup file, and enter the original passphrase that was active when it was made.

6. Licensing

CounselNote starts as a 30-day free evaluation. After purchase, you'll receive a licence key by email — paste it into Settings & safety → Licence key. CounselNote is offline software and doesn't check this against a server; it's a record of your purchase, not a copy-protection mechanism.

I forgot my passphraseIt cannot be recovered. Start a new vault and restore your most recent encrypted backup.

Moving to a new computerTake an encrypted backup on the old machine, install CounselNote on the new one, create a new vault, then restore the backup.
