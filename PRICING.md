# CounselNote — proposed UK pricing

Prices are provisional, exclude VAT and should be validated with pilot schools before launch.

## 30-day evaluation — free

- Full Professional feature set
- No card required and no automatic renewal
- Local encrypted sample vault
- Guided setup checklist

## Practitioner — £149 per year

For one named counsellor on one managed computer.

- Unlimited pupil codes and session records
- Structured UK counselling record
- Safeguarding prompts and DSL summary
- Encrypted backup and restore
- Outcome-measure totals
- Standard email support

## Professional — £249 per year

For practitioners who need operational oversight as well as case notes.

- Everything in Practitioner
- Action-owner and deadline queue
- De-identified service reports and CSV export
- Outcome trends
- Configurable school contacts and retention wording
- Priority support and annual product update

## School Assurance — £595 per year

For up to five separately encrypted practitioner installations. This edition does not create a shared clinical database.

- Everything in Professional
- Five named practitioner licences
- UK deployment, DPIA and retention-policy templates
- Remote onboarding session
- Annual safeguarding-configuration review
- Procurement documentation and invoice payment

## Trust or local authority — quotation

- Volume licensing
- Standardised configuration pack
- Train-the-trainer session
- Deployment and support agreement
- Optional custom reporting definitions

## Differentiation

The commercial distinction is not based on storing more sensitive data. Higher tiers add governance, operational reporting, workflow assurance, training and support.

Features such as cloud AI transcription, generic chatbots and automatic diagnostic conclusions are deliberately excluded from the current product. Any future assistive drafting feature should run locally, require human review and never send counselling narrative to a third-party model without an explicit, approved data-protection arrangement.
