Built for the session record, not adapted to it

Paper files get lost. Generic note-taking apps weren't built for safeguarding. CounselNote was designed from the session record outwards.

Stays on your computer

Every pupil record is encrypted with AES-GCM and stored locally. No account, no server, no cloud sync to worry about.

Built for safeguarding

Risk levels, decision rationale and a one-click DSL summary that shares only what's necessary — never the full process notes.

Outcomes you can show

GAD-7, PHQ-9/PHQ-A, SDQ and WEMWBS scoring with range checks, plus de-identified reports for leadership.

CounselNote is a private counselling workspace, not a replacement for your school's statutory child-protection system. Safeguarding concerns are always also recorded in your school's approved system, following your school's procedure.

Recognised outcome measures

GAD-7 — anxiety PHQ-9 / PHQ-A — depression SDQ — strengths & difficulties WEMWBS — wellbeing
